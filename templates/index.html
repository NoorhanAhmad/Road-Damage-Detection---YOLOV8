How it works
STEP 01
Start the backend
Run python main.py in your project folder. FastAPI starts on port 8000 and loads your trained best.pt model.
STEP 02
Upload a road photo
Take any road photo with your phone or camera and upload it here. Works with JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats up to 20 MB.
STEP 03
See the results
YOLOv8 runs inference and returns bounding boxes drawn over detected cracks and potholes, with confidence scores per class.
